THRUST

Transdisciplinary Hybrid Rocket University Students' Training

THRUST is a project aimed towards the designing, testing and launching of a small scale hybrid rocket. We are students eager to learn by doing. This project is aimed at simulating a space mission with the resources of University students.

About Us

We started as a group of Master Students in Aerospace Engineering at the University of Padova. The aim of the project is to design, develop, build and launch a small rocket that will compete in EuRoC.
Our long-term goals are to provide a solid ground for future students that will keep the project alive and to start what we hope can be a long-lasting learning experience for others.
We are backed by professors Daniele Pavarin and Francesco Barato.

University of Padova


Founded in 1222, Università degli studi di Padova has always taken great pride in the freedom of thought and expression granted to her students and professors. This freedom also takes the form of a financial backing to a great number and variety of students' projects, including ours.

University Student Project Initiative
Department of Industrial Engineering

Dipartimento di Ingegneria Industriale has always had a constant focus on providing students and professors with facilities and tools to cover the basic needs of practical and technological projects such as Thrust. The help of the Department greatly accelerated the initial phases of design.

SPONSOR
As a spin-off of the Space Propulsion group of the University of Padova, T4i was founded in 2006 with the goals of introducing space propulsion in Padova and creating a bridge between engineering students and the industry.
